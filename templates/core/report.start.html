Step 1. Select a site

The first step in starting a stepping stone garden is finding a suitable site. Through this tool, you can review the suitability of a site that you might be considering. All you have to do is select the location on a map, and the system will give you insights into the suitability of your site.

Instant site analysis

Are you keen to learn what the original vegetation type is of your own garden, your nearby community garden, or a potential plot? What plants you should start the garden with?
